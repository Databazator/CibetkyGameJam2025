# Kinds

1. Basic enemy
   - shoots straight
   - medium speed
   - medium cooldown
2. Spray enemy
   - shoots in a cone, 3-5 bullets
   - medium speed
   - longer cooldown
3. Burst enemy
   - shoots in a row, bursts of bullets
   - medium speed
   - longer cooldown
4. All rounder enemy
   - shoots in a circle all around at once, AOE basically
   - slow
   - long cooldown
5. Quickster enemy
   - shoots one bullet
   - fast
   - quick cooldown
